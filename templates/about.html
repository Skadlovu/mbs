About Us

Makamole Business Services is a South African company that provides a range of services to small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs).

  • Our Business
  • Our Mission
  • Our Vision

Makamole Business Services offers a variety of services to SMEs, including business administration, compliance and governance, cybersecurity, and data protection.

Business Administration

The company's business administration services include bookkeeping, payroll management, and office management

Compliance and Governance

Compliance and governance services help SMEs stay compliant with all relevant regulations and legislation, including tax compliance and company secretarial services.

Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity services include vulnerability assessments, penetration testing, and cyber incident response, and their data protection services help SMEs protect their data and the privacy of their customers.

Our mission is to empower mSMEs by providing them with the necessary tools and support to ensure that their businesses are compliant, secure, and sustainable.

Our vision is to be the leading provider of business administration, compliance and governance, cybersecurity, and data protection services to mSMEs in South Africa.
